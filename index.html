Why use a digital menu?


Digital restaurant menus can open up a whole new world of opportunities for your restaurant with striking visuals, a fun and interactive menu, and engaging descriptions that are easy to read, so that your diners can fully immerse themselves in a world of food, flavors, and scents!

Features



Menu Management

With an easy to use web interface, create as many menus as you need, change or add items at any point of time and publish instantly to your tablets.

Design Customization

Choose your own theme, color, and design. Create a unique looking menu that emphasizes the essence of your restaurant and the dishes you serve.

Order Traking

Your guests can make instant orders on the tablet that are sent directly to the dashboard, to your POS or to your kitchen printers right away.

Feedback Collection

Collect instant feedback from your guests and prevent your staff from tampering with the information since they are not printed anymore. .
